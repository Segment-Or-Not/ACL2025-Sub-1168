This is the story of Javier, who thinks that he is the reason behind the attack on androids,, having a conversation with his dad who is hiding. The dad assures Javier that he is safe and with Javier's mom, and checks in on Javier. Javier replies the same and confirming that he is with his sister, Wendy, and wife, Mary. Javier is ensured that dad will fix it once the attacks calm down. Javier kisses his wife goodbye before venturing out with Wendy to keep her safe, and to put an end to what is happening.
