This text discusses a futuristic "Neverland" scenario where a computer company has an android development problem. This problem results in defective androids with serious processing issues. The story protagonist finds himself as an android back in his childhood home, with his sister and his high school girlfriend. This girlfriend became his wife, and together their company  developed androids - a project that went awry costing billions of dollars. As an android, he was developed differently  - with a true heart that felt love and other emotions. The text leaves the reader wondering if we are real or androids programmed to live our lives, feeling emotions, sharing time with family and cooking bacon to perfection.
