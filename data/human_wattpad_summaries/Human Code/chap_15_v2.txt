Javier is a human android hybrid that embarks on a mission to rescue someone named Wendy. While doing this, he pushed away Mary as she tried to stop him out of her concern. Javier enters an abandoned warehouse filled with deactivated droids and memories of his past, only to sadly power down unexpectedly. Though a flashback reveals the truth that Mary who was once his fiancée turned him into a cyborg after an accident. She did this to preserve his brain, though she sacrificed his humanity. Javier now struggles with the realization that Mary had done this betrayal. Mary apologizes with tears for her actions, leaving Javier to confront his new life.
