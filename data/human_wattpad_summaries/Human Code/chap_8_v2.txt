Javi and Mary are determined to save their sister from Androids that have taken over the city.  Javi is able to reach Wendy on the phone while rustling through the streets with Mary finding cover. Javi being part android is able to fend off the bots if needed. Wendy tells Javi that the Androids have hurt 3 teachers and that Mr. Lanso, although not sure his direct relationship, is also in danger.  When Javi discovers that the androids may resync to his systems and attack, he and Mary dash for the car and head to Harris Elementary school to save Wendy.
