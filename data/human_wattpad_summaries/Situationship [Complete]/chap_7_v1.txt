This story is about a person being consumed by the memories of a past sexual encounter. They are dealing with pressure from their peers to sleep with more and more women. It seems that sex is everything this person thinks about. After working out they notice that they have a mixed text from a character named Teagan whom they have been fantasizing about. They agree to meet Teagan and are pleasantly surprised to learn that Teagan had been drafting a contract which laid out rules for an ongoing and regular sexual relationship. The contract had rules to kept the relationship to only sex and would be void if the relationship evolved into anything else, or if certain other conditions were met. The narrator can't believe their luck and happily agrees and then is even more happy to find out that the contract would be starting that very day.
