  The story is written from the perspective of a girl named Rose.  She was running a campaign with the help of a young man named Blake, who she fell in love with.  However on the night she was going to reveal her feelings for him Rose found out that an old flame of hers, Chase, had paid Blake to betray some of her campaign strategies.  
  Rose finds this out during a concert/party and leaves in tears with Blake following.  He apologizes and tells her he betrayed her for money, but it was because he did not know her well enough when he agreed to the betrayal.  Rose wavers between forgiving Blake and leaving him..she leaves.
