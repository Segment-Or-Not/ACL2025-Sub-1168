The story begins with the main character, Astrid, making a discovery. She seems to have found a boy of great significance, however he is either knowingly or unknowingly taking some sort of life force energy from a girl, making her sick. Astrid seems to now understand that the girl's name is Abel. In the world these characters live in, there are elemental threads which seem to relate to how people exist within one another's lives. Astrid suspects that the boy is in control of the seven elemental threads and refers to him as living Author. She goes to Treason's Tower to interrogate a scribe named Serah. Serah cannot speak as it seems someone cut out her tongue. Astrid uses her magical powers to intimidate Serah into giving up information regarding the living Author.
