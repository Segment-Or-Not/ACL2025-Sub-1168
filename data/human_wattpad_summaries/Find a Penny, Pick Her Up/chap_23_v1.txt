Thanksgiving is approaching, and Penny is debriefing about the secrets Annie revealed. She meets Dr. Yao, who shares her thoughts on the situation. Penny feels better about herself and her relationship with her mother, Annie. They discuss the Thanksgiving meal, which includes potatoes, cranberries, green beans, corn bread, dinner rolls, salad, and broccoli casserole. Penny's mom invites both Annie and her mother to Thanksgiving dinner, but they are surprised by the situation. They decide to make it an I-Hate-Penny party, with the Mets and Jane as guests. Penny and her mother feel confused and left behind, leaving Dr. Yao's office early.
