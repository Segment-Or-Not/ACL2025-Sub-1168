During Thanksgiving a group of family and friends, including the principle of Royal High School, get caught up in an emotional argument. Mrs. Prickett, the principle, is leaving with her daughter at the request of the rest of the family members at the table. The main character is finding the whole situation awkward and is encouraged by another character, Hampton, to take a step outside. There Hampton is surprised to see his father, Asher, who seems to have arrived unexpectedly probably after leaving from jail. Asher's reception is not warm and the night's awkwardness continues.
