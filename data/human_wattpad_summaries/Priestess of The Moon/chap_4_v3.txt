The main characters are Li Xiang, Rui, Yang Jun and Zhu Hua. Mei is also there and she's a maid. Li Xiang is the priestess of the moon, isolated by her duties and Rui, Zhu Hua, and Yang Jun try to persuade her to go outside, challenging her to a game to either stay inside or leave. Essentially all of them form a group that pushes Li Xiang to question her life? They do offer her protection however. The location is in the Tang kingdom within a palace that Li Xiang resides in, and is restricted in.
