It explains the experience of a priestess named Li Xiang, from her point of view, of going to a festivity called Chang'an fair. She is going along with General Yang Jun, Lady Zhu Hua (or Zhu Sha) and Rui. They are leaving the palace to visit the fair and this denotes that they are from a ruling class. Li Xiang has never been outside or seen this much crowd in her lifetime. She was amazed to see all the different clothing, buildings and was in love with the outside world.
It also adds the details of how much love Rui has for Li Xiang, more like an obsession to give Li anything and everything in the world. During this visit, Rui buys from fried food for the group to try. Li is enjoying the feeling of Rui eating the candied food from her hand.
